ICAIF 2025 TUTORIAL

DECISION-FOCUSED LEARNING.

Bridging Prediction and Optimization in Financial Optimization

Learn how Decision-Focused Learning directly aligns machine learning training objectives with the quality of downstream decisions. Through hands-on exercises with PyTorch and cvxpylayers, practice the techniques that achieve superior financial decisions.

Key Topics

From Theory to Practice in Decision-Focused Learning

01

DFL Foundations

Overview of the paradigm, mathematical underpinnings, and key conceptual distinctions from prediction-focused learning.

02

PyTorch Implementation

Hands-on coding with cvxpylayers for differentiable convex optimization and gradient backpropagation in neural networks.

03

Mean-Variance Optimization

Apply DFL to Markowitz portfolio optimization, comparing PFL vs DFL prediction models with real market data.

04

Goal-Based Investment

Personalized investment strategies incorporating goal programming methodology and priority optimization.

05

Partial Index Tracking

Replicate benchmark performance with limited assets using semidefinite relaxation techniques.

06

Future Directions

Open research challenges, emerging applications, and integration opportunities in financial AI.

Tutorial Schedule

Session 1

Introduction & Motivation

DFL vs PFL overview, financial applications motivation, key conceptual distinctions

Background in Decision-Focused Learning

Theoretical considerations, backpropagation challenges, PyTorch hands-on with cvxpylayers

DFL in Mean-Variance Optimization

Session 4

Goal-Based Investment & Partial Index Tracking

Goal programming methodology, index replication with limited assets, hands-on implementation

Session 5

Closing Remarks

Open research challenges, future research directions, Q&A session
